Gurantee

Guaranteed rent

If the property is vacant or the tenant fails to pay the rent, you still get paid. You will know exactly what will arrive in your bank account each month for the duration of the contract.

Same day rent payment

We pay your rent on the same day, giving you the peace of mind when working out budgets or just ensuring that the mortgage is paid on time. There is no uncertainty, as we are your professional tenants.

No fees or commission

There are no charges for setting the contract up, no tenant introduction fees, no commissions and absolutely no hidden extras to pay.
